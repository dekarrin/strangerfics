Exile Project

I've received numerous emails, and I will try to respond to them all, but it has been a busy couple of months. Without going into too many details, I've been doing some training for volunteer work, I fractured my wrist (which really makes typing fun), and I've been writing other stuff.

Other stuff??

Yes, but don't get too upset. I just finished an article for SysAdmin magazine on installing and administering Jabber. If all goes well, it will be published in January. If you're more immediately curious about my technical writing skills, read one of my other articles "Jumpstart Begin/Finish Scripts" in the October issue of SysAdmin magazine.

I do want to finish the Myst3 story, so I will try to give it priority. On the realistic side, SysAdmin magazine pays better, and I would like to use them as a way to break into writing for O'Reilly or other cool tech publishers. I promise that I will keep the work in balance.

All Exile updates will be posted to this page.
